Teaching Strategy Recommendations
Visual Learners
Use concept maps, flowcharts, color-coded notes, and diagrams. Present PNLE content as visual algorithms and decision trees.
Aural Learners
Incorporate group discussions, verbal case presentations, podcasts, and recorded lectures for review.
Read/Write Learners
Provide written outlines, structured notes, reading lists, and written case studies. Encourage note rewriting.
Kinesthetic Learners
Use simulation, skills lab practice, case-based learning, and clinical application questions. Skills Cube integration recommended.
